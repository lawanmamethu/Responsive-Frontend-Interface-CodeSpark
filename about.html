Our Mission

At DecodeLabs, we're on a mission to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands. We believe that practical, project-based training is the most effective way to become a skilled developer. Our goal is to help you build a portfolio that speaks louder than a resume.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in Greater Lucknow, DecodeLabs started with a simple idea: coding education should be accessible, practical, and industry-aligned.

What began as a small mentorship group has grown into a full-fledged training institute with over 500+ successful graduates placed in top tech companies across India.

Our founders—themselves experienced full stack developers—saw that traditional computer science education often left students unprepared for real-world development. So they built DecodeLabs to fix that.
